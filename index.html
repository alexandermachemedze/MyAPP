ABOUT

GraniteConstruction is a well-established Zimbabwean contracting and industrial group, providing innovative engineering and infrastructure client solutions to the agriculture, commercial, communications, housing, mining, water and transport sectors.

GraniteConstruction can provide complete fit-out & design services for any type of business, from custom built fit-outs to upgrades & skill-full of existing premises. We have extensive experience in producing quality fit-outs, on time & on budget, for high profile national brands, franchise businesses as well as unique specialty stores.
